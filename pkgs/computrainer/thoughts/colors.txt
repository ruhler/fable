Colors
======

Things that need colors:
* Each kind of car: C, 0, 1, S, L
* The background
* Generic gate coloring.

That's 7 colors to start with. I should pick ones that look nice together.

From what I read on the internet:
* Colors X and Y are complementary if Y = White - X.
* Complementary colors contrast really well.
* It's nice to have three variations on the color for an object:
  base, shade (darkened base), and tint (lightened base).

What colors can we derive from complements? Well, seems like we can subtract
colors as an operation. Start with White and some arbitrary color like blue.
You can get:

  W, X, Y = (W - X), Z = Y - X?

Not sure that's going to get me very far.
