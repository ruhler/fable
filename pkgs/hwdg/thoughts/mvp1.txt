verify it gives the right result. Don't worry about gui yet.

But it makes sense to think a little bit about how we are going to map ports
to coordinates to draw. Here's the state I'm thinking about:

Data@ or Bit@: 0 or 1. Let's say Data@.
Port@ - unique id of a port.
Memory@ - map from Port@ to Data@.

Can we come up with a better name for Port@? Addr@? Sure. Addr@ works for me.

So, we have:
* Data@, Addr@, Memory@
* Rule@, Design@
